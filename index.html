Dental Office Location Finder

This project started from my uncle's request to find the perfect location to open new dental clinics. Although many businesses could transition into intact services, it is different for dental clinics. People have to go to dentists physically to get the therapy they need. Therefore, location is crucial for profit maximization. I've considered many factors for the perfect location, such as population and competitor dental clinics, and put them in my machine learning model to predict the profit for the location.
